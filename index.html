PROJECTS

Logo Design

For this project, I designed a logo inspired by my classmate's passions and interests. Drawing from her love for photography and her desire to evoke nostalgia and joy, I created a design that celebrates her journey in capturing memories through visual art. Using Adobe Illustrator, I crafted a logo that embodies the warmth and vibrance of summer, aligning with her vision and story.

JavaScript Game

For this project, I developed a Whack-a-Mole game from scratch using JavaScript. The game challenges players to test their reflexes with increasing difficulty, as the speed intensifies every 10 successful hits. Players have five chances to miss before the game ends, adding a layer of strategy and excitement. This project allowed me to explore dynamic game mechanics and refine my programming skills.

Nature Portrait

This silent short film captures the beauty of nature, emphasizing its tranquility and serenity. The documentary features fields of grass, trees adorned with vibrant leaves, and small creatures like butterflies and ladybugs, alongside diverse flowers and plants. It also showcases people strolling along trails and enjoying the park’s peaceful ambiance. Through these visuals, the film conveys a deep connection to nature and celebrates the simple joys of spending time outdoors.
